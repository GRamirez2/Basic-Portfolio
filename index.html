About Me

Specializing in photographing architecture and interior environments, George's work captures the spirit and sense of a space and seeks to create interiors that feel intimate and private. He also welcomes all editorial assignments, as he is a storyteller at heart.

George earned a BFA from the University of Texas at San Antonio while working as a portrait photographer. He continued his education receiving a MFA in photography from the University of North Texas. As a fine arts photographer, George has produced several bodies of work that are in public and private collections including the Museum of Fine Arts, Houston, McNeese State University, and the University of North Texas.

George's interest in art history led him to a career in the museum field where he spent 10 years working at the Museum of Fine Arts, Houston developing digital media projects and public programs. Surrounded by fine art, this experience introduced George to world-renowned curators, photographers, artists, and other experts who shaped his world-view and his current work.

Currently George lives in Austin, Texas with his beautiful wife and "the girls", Lilly & Patti, their two Bichon Frises. In his spare time keeps-up on the latest in photographic lighting equipment and post-production technologies. He is obsessed with honing his Pandora stations.
